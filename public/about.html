Welcome to Swaranjali Motors

Founded by Sagar Shinde, Swaranjali Motors has been serving Sangli and surrounding areas since 2018. What started as a small venture has grown into one of the most trusted names in the second-hand vehicle market in the region.

Our commitment to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction has helped us build lasting relationships with hundreds of happy customers. We specialize in commercial vehicles including trucks, pickups, and also offer cars and two-wheelers.
